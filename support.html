Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

What are the system requirements for Osinters?

Osinters is a web-based platform, so all you need is a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and a stable internet connection.

How can I upgrade my subscription plan?

You can upgrade your subscription plan from your account dashboard under the "Billing" or "Subscription" section.
