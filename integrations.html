Platform Integrations

Seamlessly connect Osinters with your favorite tools and data sources.

Our Integration Philosophy

Osinters is designed to be a central hub for your open-source intelligence activities. We believe in the power of interoperability and strive to make it easy for you to connect our platform with the other tools and services you rely on. This allows for a more streamlined workflow and enhances your investigative capabilities.

Available Integrations

We are constantly expanding our list of integrations. Here are some of the key categories and examples:

  • Data Enrichment Services: Connect to services that provide additional context and data points for your findings (e.g., IP geolocation, domain reputation).
  • Threat Intelligence Platforms: Feed your OSINT findings into your existing TIPs or pull threat actor information into Osinters.
  • Case Management Systems: Export structured data from Osinters directly into your case management software.
  • Custom API Access: Utilize our API to build custom integrations tailored to your specific needs.
  • Browser Extensions: Leverage our browser extensions for quick information gathering and integration with web-based sources.

For a detailed list of currently supported integrations and setup guides, please visit our developer portal or contact our support team.

Requesting New Integrations

Is there a tool or service you'd like to see integrated with Osinters? We'd love to hear from you! Please submit your integration requests through our contact page or community forum.
